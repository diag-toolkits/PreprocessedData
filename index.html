The data matrices containing histograms of TACC Stats resource use counters by one-hour time-bins can be downloaded here and the link above. Each row in the matrix represents a resource use counter. Each column in the matrix represents a one hour time-bin.

A description of the analyses utilising the TACC_Stats resource use data can be found in the paper listed below:

(1) E. Chuah, A. Jhumka, J.C. Browne, N. Gurumdimma, S. Narasimhamurthy, B. Barth, Using Message Logs and Resource Use Data for Cluster Failure Diagnosis, In Proceedings of the 23rd IEEE International Conference on High Performance Computing, Data, and Analytics (HiPC '16), 19-22 December, 2016. DOI: 10.1109/HiPC.2016.20

Acknowledgement: This work is supported by The Alan Turing Institute under the EPSRC grant EP/N510129/1 and the National Science Foundation under OCI awards #0622780 and #1203604 to The Texas Advanced Computing Center at the University of Texas at Austin.
